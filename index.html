Why Choose us

Make your car feel like a brand new one.

COST

A dime size dent in your car door can cost up to 3-5 times more to repair at a body shop compared to using PDR.

TIME & CONVENIENCE

PDR can be done at your home (in your driveway) or office (in the parking lot). An average quarter-size dent can take 20 minutes to an hour typically. The same dent at some body shops can leave you without your car for 2-4 days.

VALUE

You retain the value of your car. The dents are not filled with putty and then re-painted (which is the traditional style.) When a dent is removed correctly, your vehicle is actually returned to its original form. Your car will also not require being re-painted!
